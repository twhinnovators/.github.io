Unveiling a New Era of Innovation: "The White Hole" Robotics Club's Grand Opening





Chattogram, February 13, 2024

In a momentous event that resonates with the spirit of innovation and collaboration, "The White Hole" Robotics Club officially marked its beginning with a grand opening ceremony on February 13, 2024. The ceremony, held at CDA Avenue, East Nasirabad, Chattogram, brought together an assembly of passionate individuals dedicated to the field of robotics.

The event commenced with a soulful recitation from the Holy Quran, setting a tone of reverence and unity. This was followed by the national anthem, symbolizing pride and patriotism. As the program unfolded, attendees were treated to a rich array of activities, speeches, and future visions that promise to make "The White Hole" Robotics Club a hub for technological enthusiasts.

The Chief Advisor, Professor Md. Jashim Uddin Khan, Principal of Bakalia Government College, shared his insights on the importance of robotics in education and the potential of the club to become a center for cutting-edge technological advancements.

President Md. Tanvir Rahman, a distinguished figure in the field, shared his achievements, which include being the District and Divisional Champion and National Runner-Up at "Banabandhu National Talent Hunt-2023," Champion in "National Environmental Olympiad-2023," "National Science Carnival-2023," "Expeditious-2023," "National Child-Youth Assembly-2021, 2022, 2023," "National Child Award-2020" and others. His illustrious achievements speak volumes about his commitment to excellence and leadership in the realm of robotics.

In his opening speech, President Tanvir expressed his excitement about the journey ahead. He highlighted the club's commitment to providing a platform for robotics enthusiasts, organizing campaigns, and hosting Robotics Olympiads to discover and nurture talent.

The Vice President, Mubasshira Rownak Kabir, spoke passionately about the club's mission and its dedication to fostering a vibrant robotics community. Other committee members presented their roles and shared their enthusiasm for the club's future endeavors.

One of the key moments of the ceremony was the announcement of the club's advisors: Professor Md. Jashim Uddin Khan as the Wisdom and Strategy Advisor, Syed Sharfuddin Ishtiaque Antha as the Community Relations Advisor, and Rony Mitra as the Senior Technical Advisor.

The program included a special segment to appreciate the advisors, where they were presented with honorary gestures as tokens of gratitude for their commitment to the club's success.

As the evening unfolded, members took an oath, pledging their commitment to the club's principles and goals. The ceremony concluded with a vibrant closing, leaving everyone inspired and excited about the future of "The White Hole" Robotics Club.

The club's founder and President, Md. Tanvir Rahman, expressed his gratitude to everyone who attended and contributed to the success of the opening ceremony. "This marks the beginning of an incredible journey. With the support of our dedicated committee members and esteemed advisors, I am confident that 'The White Hole' Robotics Club will be a beacon of innovation and collaboration," he remarked.

The grand opening was not just an event; it was a declaration of the club's commitment to shaping the future of robotics in Chattogram and beyond. With a dynamic leadership team and a passionate community, "The White Hole" Robotics Club is poised to become a hub for technological excellence and a driving force for innovation in the region.
